Our vision

“Education and empowerment of women in the world can only lead to a more altruistic, tolerant, just and peaceful life for all”.
(Aung San Suu Kyi, Nobel Peace Prize laureate).

We believe that the gender gap in Academia is a phenomenon that is still little known, and we intend to spread awareness of it through scientific education and outreach activities.

Our mission

Our goal is to break down gender stereotypes in Academia by providing appropriate training, positive role models and support for women while respecting their fundamental rights.
